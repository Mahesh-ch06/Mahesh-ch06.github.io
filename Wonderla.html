    Overview

    Breathtaking water slides

    The park was set up in 2000 and was designed by architect Joseph John. Wonderla Kochi is the first park in India to get ISO 14001 certificate for eco-friendliness and OHSAS 18001 certificate for safety. The park is spread over 30 acres of landscaped space with more than 60 amusement rides

    Water pools for those who are looking for some relaxation under the sun, there are exhilarating, and adrenaline-pumping rides for those looking for adventure and there are rides for casual fun and frolic as well.
